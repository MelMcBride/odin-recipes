Taco

A taco is a Mexican dish that typically consists of a corn or wheat tortilla that is filled with meat, cheese, vegetables, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Beef faijita(or any meat of choice)
  • Corn or flower tortias
  • vegtable or frying oil
  • Seasoning
  • butter
  • Queso or any mexican style cheese
  • onion
  • tomato
  • Cilantro
  • lime
  • avacado
  • Store bought salsa or homemade salsa(option)
  • sour cream(option)

Steps

  1. Season beef fajitas and put on a heated grill
  2. Grill until faijias are cooked too your liking
  3. Chop an onion into C-shaped peices and grill as well
  4. Dice tomato,onion and cilantro and toss in a bowl with lime juice
  5. Cut ripe avacodo in half and scoop out, throw away the seed and put the avacado in a bowl. Then season the avacodo and put half of the diced tomato,onion,and cilantro and mix.
  6. put butter in a pan and grill tortia of choice
  7. when done build put everything together including sides of choice and cheese
